What All-Inclusive includes at this hotel

Breakfast 08:00–10:00 Lunch 13:00–15:00 Dinner 19:00–21:00 Poolside bar 09:00–19:00

All meals are served buffet style. Drinks included with meals: draught juices, coffee, tea, still and sparkling water, draught beer, draught white/rosé/red wine, vodka, liqueur, whisky. At the poolside bar: juices, coffee, tea, water, draught beer and wine.

On the first day the first meal is dinner, and on departure day the last meal is lunch. The buffet is set up for a minimum of 60 guests; below that, a set menu with options to choose from.

It also includes access to the pools with sunbeds and parasols, 3 playgrounds, a tennis court with equipment and free Wi-Fi throughout the complex.
